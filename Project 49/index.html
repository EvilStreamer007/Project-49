Interesting facts about the Camel

  1. Camels ears are furry
  2. Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specifically designed feet
  3. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.

Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting facts about Bears

  1. There are around 180,000 to 200,000 brown bears worldwide.
    -Most of them live in Alaska, Canada and Russia where the brown bear can still roam vast areas almost unpopulated by human beings and covered in forests.
  2. The brown bear can reach a weight of between 150 and 370 kilogrammes depending on age, gender and season.
  3. Despite their weight, the animals can cover short distances at speeds of up to 50 km/h.
